Subject Teacher
Instructions:
1.Subject and Teacher should only be initials.The teacher should have already been present in the database.
2.For Subjects handled by other departments(Ex POM,MATH etc) mention teacher name as OT
3.For Lab subjects the format should be SubjectLab(Batch) Ex:  USPLab(B1)
4.For Lab subjects the teacher format should be with a '+' sign Ex:   GRS+MM+NKC
5.Use cntrl+f to remove all spaces in the end.(Very Important)
